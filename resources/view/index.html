Synopsis

Welcome to Dog Rescue Shelter System

A Dog Rescue center is looking to streamline its existing paper-based application procedure.

How our rehoming process works:

1.Create an account and fill out our application form.

2.Select the dog you want to adopt from the homepage

3.Fill out the application form and wait for approval, if approved proceed to the next step otherwise you need to re-apply

4.After approval, schedule an appointment for an interview, and wait for the email notification

5.After the interview, if you are approved, you can take the dog home
